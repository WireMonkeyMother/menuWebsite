What to use

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Flour

How to use it

  1. In the mixing bowl of a stand mixer, combine flour, salt, and sugar
  2. Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and add in eggs, milk, and vanilla
  3. Using paddle attachment, beat until just combined. Be careful not to overmix
  4. Scoop out 2 tablespoons at a time into round balls, placing them onto a greased baking sheet (8 per sheet)
  5. Bake in oven at 350 for 10 minutes, then remove and smack the tray on the counter. Return to oven for another 10 minutes
  6. Let cool on a wire baking rack for at least 15 minutes for maximum enjoyment!
