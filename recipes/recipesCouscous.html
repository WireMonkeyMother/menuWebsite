What to use

  • 2/3 cup dry couscous
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 tsp chicken bouilon or 2 tsp beef bouilon
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

How to use it

  1. In a small saucepan, boil the water with the bouilon of your choice
  2. Once the broth is boiling, add in the oil and dry couscous
  3. Stir and remove from heat, then let sit for 5 minutes before serving
