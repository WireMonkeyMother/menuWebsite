What to use

  • 4 or 5 fresh avacados, cut into large chunks
  • 3 roma tomatoes, chopped into 1 inch cubes
  • The juice and zest of 1-2 limes (depending on preference)
  • 1 jalapeno or serrano pepper, finly chopped
  • 2-3 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 2-3 tbsp finely chopped red onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to use it

  1. Put all ingredients into a large mixing bowl and stir until you reach your desired consistency, adding more salt as needed
