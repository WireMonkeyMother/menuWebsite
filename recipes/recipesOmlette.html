What to use

  • 2-3 eggs, thoroughly beaten
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • A pinch of pepper
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • Any combination of vegetables, cheeses, and/or meats that you like (I enjoy mushrooms, cheddar, and bacon)

How to use it

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs along with the milk, salt, and pepper
  2. Heat a skillet on medium-high heat and add oil to coat the bottom evenly
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and allow to cook for 1-2 minutes
  4. Gently, trying to avoid tearing the eggs, lift the cooked eggs up and let the still raw eggs flow underneath
  5. Add any meat, cheeses, or vegetables you want to one half of the cooked eggs
  6. Turn the heat to medium-low, lace a lid on the skillet and allow to cook for 1-2 minutes
  7. If firm enough, fold the omlette in half and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until the outside is just starting to darken
  8. It's okay if the inside is still soft when you are done, it will continue to cook for another few minutes after you take it off the heat
