What to use

  • 2 heads fresh broccoli cut into florets (approximately 1 lb)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

How to use it

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 F
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, toss the broccoli florets with the oil, salt and pepper
  3. Place oiled broccoli on a baking sheet, spreading evenly into a single layer
  4. Roast in the oven for 25-35 minutes, until just starting to char around the edges
  5. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before enjoying
