What to use

  • 3/4 cup ground almonds
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon dry thyme
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 4 boneless, skinless, chicken breast, 4 ounces each
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

How to use it

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Lightly spray a baking sheet with nonstick spray or place down a silicone baking pad
  3. In a large pie pan or other wide shallow dish, combine almonds, flour, thyme, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper
  4. Pour the milk into a separate smaller bowl
  5. Place each chicken piece into the flour mixture and coat evenly, then dip it into the milk and soak all sides, then back into the flour mixture and coat evenly again
  6. Place all chicken pieces on baking sheet and place in oven for 25 minutes, ensuring that the internal temperature of the thickest piece reaches at least 165 F
  7. Using hot mitts, remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the chicken to cool 5-10 minutes before enjoying.
